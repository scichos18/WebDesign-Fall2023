My Life As A Horse Nerd

By Samantha Cichos

I started riding horses when I was seven years old, and it has become my passion. My family currently owns two horses, Austin and Baylie, and we regularly attend horse shows. I would definitely call myself a "horse nerd" - I love learning about anything related to horses, horse showing, and horse care.


Austin and Baylie begging for treats.


The barn in Snoqualmie where the horses live and where I take riding lessons.

I am always trying to improve my riding skills so I can be more competitive at horse shows, and so I can continue my riding career at the collegiate level. Additionally, I take great pride in caring for my horses at the highest level possible and ensuring they are happy and healthy, so I am always trying to learn about new techniques relating to horse care (as well as always buying more treats and toys). On this website, you will find some basic information regarding horse knowledge and maintenance, as well as a look into my life as a horse nerd.
